Breathe in, Breathe out Who is Dalla

Dalla Cafe was the idea of two friends who wished to build a cafe that will have a tranquil atmosphere. With tranquility comes positive energy. It allows to get rid of negative thoughts and feelings in your life. By gaining peace and tranquility in our life, we will find ourselves taking more time to appreciate the little things and be less hurried to get anywhere.

We want you to allow your body to relax and re-energize. With local ingredients we chose, we will serve you with our best magical coffee that will heal your soul and warm your heart. #selfcare #trustinDalla
